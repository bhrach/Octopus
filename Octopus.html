Why The Octopus?

This is the noble octopus. It is assuredly the coolest creature known to humanity. Sadly, too many poor octopuses go unnoticed by society. They sullenly slink away into soda bottles to hide their shame. Please take a moment to consider your thoughts about this wonderful beast. You know you want to.
